PAINTING INSPIRATION

This painting by CSS was inpsired by the Wassily Kandinsky's "Several Circles" oil painting done in 1926. It is an abstraction of numerous circles varying in transparencies, sizes, and colors. The original painting contained some irregular contours in the background that could not translate over to the coding because of how they were shaped. In addition, some of the overlapped circles did not reflect the colors that would be made when the two colors were mixed. This is why a few of the colors are not exactly like those in the original work due to how CSS follows the basics of when two transparent circles overlap each other and form a new color, like how blue and yellow would mix to make green. Everything else is the same, and the cirlces are in the exact or very close to the positions of those in "Several Cirlces."
